Beer Sheva Mobility Study

Primary Data Source

  • •High-precision smartphone GPS data (meter-level accuracy)
  • •Aggregated by Decell and preprocessed by Adalya
  • •Bidirectional Origin-Destination data between statistical zones and POIs
Fully Anonymized & Privacy-Protected

Spatial Analysis Units

  • •CBS statistical areas for residential zones
  • •13 key Points of Interest in Beer Sheva Metro

Temporal Coverage

  • •Stage 1: November 2019 - February 2020
  • •Stage 2: July 2021 (Post-COVID comparison)
  • •Average weekday (excluding Sunday & Thursday) mobility patterns

Key Data Features

  • •Transportation modes (Car, Bus, Train, Walk, Bike)
  • •Trip purpose classification (Work vs. Other)
  • •Hourly temporal resolution
Data Quality: This dataset captures the majority of trips within the district. Sample balancing was applied during preprocessing to ensure demographic representativity, though some inherent biases may remain. All data has undergone rigorous validation by Decell and Adalya.
